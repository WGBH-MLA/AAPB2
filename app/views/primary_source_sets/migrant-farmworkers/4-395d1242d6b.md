## Introduction

This episode of Radio Bilingüe explores the ongoing struggles and contributions of California farmworkers through highlights from a series of public forums on labor rights and immigrant protections. Farmworkers, labor advocates, and legal experts discuss unsafe working conditions, wage theft, immigration enforcement, and the lasting impact of recent ICE raids on rural communities. The program also honors the legacy of organizers who fought for farmworker protections while encouraging listeners to consider the challenges agricultural workers continue to face today.
