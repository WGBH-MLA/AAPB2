## Introduction

This episode highlights Latina Entrepreneurs. Without money and some without documents, Latina immigrant women draw on their ancestral cultural traditions and community-driven practices to find the strength and tools to start new businesses and prosper against all odds. Leading women who recently met at a summit in Berkeley share ideas on how to fight old biases and taboos, help heal entire communities, and empower themselves in the financial area.
