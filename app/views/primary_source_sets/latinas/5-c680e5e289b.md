## Introduction

In this audio clip, host, Samuel Orozco, interviews activists and leaders who organized a one-day strike for women, A Day Without a Woman, on International Women’s Day. These activists and leaders include Dolores Huerta, Marcela Romero Rivera, and Nestora Salgado.
