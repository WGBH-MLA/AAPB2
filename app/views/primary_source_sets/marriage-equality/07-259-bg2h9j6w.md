## Introduction

A year after the Massachusetts Supreme Judicial Court called for the legalization of same-sex marriage, LGBTQ+ couples and organizations continued to fight for marriage rights in the courts of New Jersey. Some of these couples were profiled on this 2004 episode of Due Process, a legal issues show sponsored by the New Jersey Bar Association that aired on the New Jersey Network. The report also includes some studio commentary and street interviews that give a sense of public sentiment in 2004.
