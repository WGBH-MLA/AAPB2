Reporting from Alcatraz Island during the All Tribes occupation of the island, Trudell (Santee Sioux) interviews Sioux Indian and University of California-Berkeley student Burnell Blindman. Trudell and Blindman discuss conditions on Indian reservations, and the Alcatraz occupation and its goals.
