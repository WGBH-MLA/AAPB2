## Introduction

National Farm Workers Association leader Cesar Chavez and thousands of migrant farmworkers launch a historic strike against California grape growers in this powerful documentary about the fight for fair wages, union rights, and dignity in the fields. Filmed during the 1965 Delano grape strike, Huelga! follows organizers and workers as they face poverty, discrimination, and fierce opposition while building a movement that transformed labor rights in the United States.
