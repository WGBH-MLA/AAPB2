## About

KEDT-TV has been dedicated to educating, enlightening and inspiring South Texans since we first went on the air in October 1972. KEDT-FM signed on in March 1984.

As a dual-licensed public broadcasting station, KEDT is the vessel that helps deliver free PBS and NPR educational and cultural programming to our region, which covers South Texas from Victoria to the Rio Grande Valley — that's 27 counties and over 2 million Texans!
